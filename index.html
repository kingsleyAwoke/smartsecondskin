Smart Second Skin is an innovative wearable technology company that has developed an advanced "smart skin" solution for continuous health monitoring. Founded in 2003, our mission is to redefine personal health tracking through comfortable, seamless wearable sensors.

The Smart Second Skin material is embedded with flexible sensors that capture a range of physiological data, including heart rate, breathing, temperature, and activity. This data is transmitted wirelessly to a companion app, providing users with real-time health insights and analytics.

Beyond consumer applications, Smart Second Skin is collaborating with healthcare providers to integrate its technology into clinical settings for improved patient monitoring and care. With a talented team and advanced materials science, we are at the forefront of the wearable health revolution.
